About Me

This page is intended to introduce myself and give my audience the opportunity to see what I have accomplished while working towards becoming a web developer.

While going to college for my bachelors, like many others, I didn't really know what I wanted to do. As time passed, I knew that I had to focus on something, so I chose the field of finance and accounting. I graduated with a Bachelors in Finance in 2010, then decided to go back and get a Masters in Accounting. Since I have graduated with my masters degree, I have obtained CMA designation and accumulated 6 years of experience, mostly as a financial analyst. Not until a few years into my career as a financial analyst did I realize that I did not have enough passion for it. However, what I did realize is that I loved everything about Excel, building financial models, creating complex formulas, and creating macros for reducing the time it takes for repetitive tasks. It took a little bit of time to put myself in the right position, but I am now attending a coding bootcamp at UT Austin and know that I have made the right choice.

Thank you for taking the time to look at my material, and I hope you appreciate what you see.
